Patent description

According, to the present invention relates to smart sole shoe for posture correction of human body, comprising of a sole (1) including a lower sole (9) and an upper sole (10); and a microcontroller (3) placed inside the compartment of sole (1). The shoe (100) may include a plurality of weight pressure sensors (5) placed on the sole (1); and a vibrator (7) placed on the sole (1). Accordingly, when a user wears the shoe (100), the pressure from all the weight pressure points on each foot is collected continuously by using the plurality of pressure sensors (5). When the pressure continues to be greater than the threshold value for a certain predefined time, the vibrator (7) is initiated to let the user know that the user is applying more pressure on a particular foot for a long time.
